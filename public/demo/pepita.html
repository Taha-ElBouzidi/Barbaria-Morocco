Static gold pigment

A repeating overlay of ~100 tiny gold dots in a noise pattern, layered with mix-blend-screen. No animation. Reads like fine gold flakes suspended in dark leather. Premium and stable, never competes for attention.

B · Constellation Scatter

Twinkling Sahara sky

25 small gold four-point stars sparsely scattered, each twinkling on its own 4-second loop with randomised delays. The surface "breathes." Romantic, Amazigh-aligned. Matches the Histoire voice (campfires, the sun, the night sky pressed onto the brand).

Option C
C · Shimmer Sweep

Polished bronze glint

A diagonal radial gold gradient drifts across the surface every 7 seconds, finished with a fine noise grain on overlay. Like a slow glint passing over hand-polished bronze. Quiet luxury, most subtle of the three.

Option D · Hybrid
D · Hybrid — your pick

Gold dust caught in moving light

The diagonal radial glow from C, sliding slowly across the surface. Layered with 45 small gold pepitas of varied sizes (some four-point stars, some pinprick dots), each fading in and out on its own random rhythm. Non-uniform on purpose: positions, sizes, peak brightnesses and timings are all randomised. Reads as Sahara gold caught in a passing light.
